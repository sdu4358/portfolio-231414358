Project Summary

Vendorlution is a student-friendly marketplace inspired by social platforms but focused on safety. Buyers pay into an escrow wallet; sellers receive funds only after the buyer confirms delivery. The platform supports ID verification, buyer–seller chat, listings, and a transparent dispute flow.

Why escrow matters

It prevents instant payouts to sellers and ensures items are delivered as described before releasing funds.

Objectives

  • Provide secure, escrow-based payments for peer-to-peer trades.
  • Streamline listing discovery and messaging.
  • Establish trust via verification, ratings, and clear dispute resolution.

Key Features

  • Escrow wallet: hold-until-confirmation payouts.
  • Verified profiles and listing moderation.
  • In-app chat, order tracking, and receipts.

Non-Goals (MVP)

  • No built-in logistics network (buyers/sellers arrange shipping).
  • No auctions; fixed-price listings for simplicity.

Roadmap

Milestone 1 — Listings & Profiles

Create, edit, and browse listings; basic profile pages; seed data.

Milestone 2 — Escrow & Orders

Wallet top-ups, hold-funds on order, buyer confirmation, admin release.

Milestone 3 — Chat & Disputes

In-app messaging, dispute ticketing, resolution workflow.
